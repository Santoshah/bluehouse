Blue House Infant & Toddler Atelier

Welcome to the Infant & Toddler Atelier, a space exclusively designed to encourage discovery,exploration and interaction for children aged 6-36 months. The parent and child sessions in our ateliers are emergent instyle and may incorporate up to three elements in one hour. Each element seamlessly flows into one another and isfacilitated and guided by our experienced Blue House staff.

Explorations

Free exploration as families gather in the atelier.

Connections

where parents, children and teachers gather to share in a variety of experiences. We often invite specialist teachers from the Blue House team and the community to join us during our sessions and we shall inform you accordinglyof the dates/times that a guest will join us.

Collaboration & Wonder:

where parents and children work together, enjoying deeper explorations of theenvironment and experiences.
