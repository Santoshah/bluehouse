We have several classes and drop in sessions on offer during the week and something extra special on Saturday’s.

Creative community

A messy exploratory session that encapsulates the beauty and connection between thenatural environment and creativity. Bring a change ofclothes!

Exploration of Sound

Come and join our resident music specialist and be part of a musical project whereyou and your child will experience the wonder ofinstruments, singing, poetry and rhyme.

Reggionarra Story and Drama

Come and join our resident drama specialist who will envelop both you andyour child with wonder, imagination and creativity throughthe language and magic of story-telling- the dancebetween cognitive, expressive, rational and imaginative.

Ray of Light

Encapsulating the language of light to deepen and strengthen a child’s natural curiosity andunderstanding. Come along to experience the array oflight sources available in the atelier.

‘Daddy and Me’ (6-36 months)

60 minutes A wonderful opportunity for fathers to spend some one on one time with their children and explore the wonder of the atelier without feeling conscious that they are the only Dad in the room!

‘Family Connections’ (6-36 months)

60 minutes This session is perfect for children aged 6 to 36 months. We purposefully stage the mixed age group to accommodate families with one or more children with an opportunity to spendtime exploring in theatelier as a family.
